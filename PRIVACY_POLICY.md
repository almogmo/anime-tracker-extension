# Privacy Policy — Anime Fans Tracker

_Last updated: May 30, 2026_

Anime Fans Tracker ("the Extension") is designed with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what the Extension does and does not do with your information.

## Summary

**The Extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your own device.**

## What the Extension stores

The Extension stores the following **locally on your device** using the browser's built-in storage (`chrome.storage`):

- The anime titles and episode numbers you choose to save.
- The link (URL) of the page you were on when you saved an anime, captured so you can return to it.
- Your on/off preference for the Netflix Auto-Skip feature.

This data never leaves your browser. It is not sent to us or to any third party. There are no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no external servers.

## Permissions and why they are used

- **storage** — to save your tracked anime and settings locally on your device.
- **tabs** — to read the current tab's link when you save an anime, so the Extension can store it for quick access.
- **Host access to netflix.com** — so the Auto-Skip feature can detect and click Netflix's "Skip Intro" and end-of-episode "Next Episode" buttons while you watch. The Extension only runs on Netflix pages and does not read or modify content on any other website.

## Data sharing

We do **not** collect your data, so there is nothing to share. No information is transmitted off your device.

## Children's privacy

The Extension does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted in the Extension's public repository.
